Brew of The Moon

Description

Fighting Ahzidal or another fire-based Dragon priest? Choose Fire Salts. Need protection from a Forsworn Briarheart's frosty attacks? Moon Sugar is the way to go.

Ingredients

  • Dwarven Oil
  • Snowberries
  • Fire Salts/Moon Sugar

Steps

  1. Find Dwarven oil in Dwarven ruins.
  2. Snowberries found areas near winterhold.
  3. Defeat Flame atronarch to collect fire salt.
  4. Moon sugar from khajiit caravans only.
  5. Craft potion in alchemy lab.

Elixir of Ancestry

Description

Four-effect potions are rare in Skyrim, and even rarer are all four effects beneficial. This magic brew will get players back on their feet no matter what has them down.This potion will bring the Dragonborn back from the brink of death more resiliant than before, and give them the energy to pull off more power attacks.

Ingredients

  • Charred Skeever Hide
  • Mudcrab Chitin
  • Swamp Fungal Pod

Steps

  1. Find a roasted skeever in a bandit hideout to collect charred skeever hide.
  2. Defeat a Mudcrab
  3. Find Fungal pod in Morthal and the surrounding marshes of Hjaalmarch.
  4. Craft potion in alchemy lab.
